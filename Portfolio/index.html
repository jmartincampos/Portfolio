About Me

Hey there! I'm Jonathan Campos, a passionate full-stack developer with a love for crafting innovative solutions that make a real impact. With an unconventional background in clinical diagnostics, I've honed my skills in both front-end and back-end development, allowing me to create seamless and robust web applications from start to finish.

As a full-stack developer, I thrive in diverse technical environments and am proficient in a wide range of technologies including:

  • Front-end: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), React.js, Vue.js
  • Back-end: Node.js, Express.js, Django, Flask
  • Databases: MongoDB, PostgreSQL, MySQL
  • Version Control: Git, GitHub
  • Deployment: Heroku, AWS, Docker
  • Other Tools & Technologies: RESTful APIs, GraphQL, WebSocket, OAuth

    What truly drives me is the opportunity to solve complex problems and create meaningful solutions that have a positive impact on people's lives. Whether it's streamlining processes, enhancing user experiences, or driving business growth, I'm motivated by the challenge of pushing boundaries and turning ideas into reality.
